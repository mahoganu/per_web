My name is Robert Kenworthy


I'm a student at Rice University in Houston, Texas, studying Electrical Engineering with a concentration in Data Science, expecting to graduate in '23.

My interest do not soley lie within the broad field of Electrical Engineering. I have a passion for design, machine learning, and the collection, processing, and visualization of data. I seek to experience and develop these altogether with a focus on how machine learning and data collection can be optimized and utilized for the design and development of autonomous devices or algorithms. I realized during my first two years at school, most of the physical aspect of design has been greatly modulated and only a more intense field such as research would consistently require me to use the more tradition EE skills I have learned. Because of this, I have found a greater attraction to the software side of design, since software allows much more flexibility and less lag time to see the final product than compared to hardware design. I am still branching out and discovering new niches everyday, and pushing myself to experience as much as I can within the Venn Diagram containing electrical engineering and computer science topics.

I've also an interest in business and investing, along with the requisite skills in leadership, business planning, public speaking, and presentation. I love talking to and learning about new people, along with learning the histories and stories behind things like the languages we speak or countries we live in. I've got a lot of interests and not enough time to explore them all.
